Why Do We Have a Privacy Policy?

We have developed this Privacy Policy to ensure that you feel confident about the privacy and security of your personal information. When we refer to “personal information” in this policy, we mean information that identifies you as an individual or is capable of doing so.

Accordingly, we may hold and use data about you in your capacity as a consumer, a business customer, an individual acting on behalf of a business customer, a shareholder, or in any other capacity, including when you visit our websites.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By visiting our website or using any of our services, you indicate your agreement to our use of your personal information as described in this policy.

What Does This Privacy Policy Cover?

This Privacy Policy applies only to how Shree Adhinath Merchantile deals with your personal information. It does not apply to any other company or to any other company’s websites, even if you access them through our website.

If you disclose your personal information to other companies, your information will be handled in accordance with their own privacy practices.

What Personal Information Do We Collect and When?

We may collect personal information to enable us to provide our services to you. This information may be collected via telephone, written correspondence, or through our website.

The information we collect may include your name, residential or business address, contact telephone numbers, email address, and payment details where required.

We may also request additional information related to the services you are using or ordering. When participating in competitions or promotions, we may request information that will be clearly explained at the time of entry.

We will always inform you of the purposes for which the information you provide may be used.

Website Usage Information

We collect information from visitors to our websites to help us improve website functionality and services. This includes information such as the number of visitors, visit duration, and pages viewed.

This information is aggregated and anonymous, meaning individual users cannot be identified. We may share this data with advertisers or partners to demonstrate the effectiveness of our website as a sales and marketing channel.

We may also monitor and record communications with you, including emails and phone conversations, for training, quality assurance, record-keeping, and legal or regulatory compliance.

Information Collected from Third Parties

We may receive personal information about you from other companies or organizations, such as marketing partners. In such cases, we rely on those third parties to have obtained your consent to share your information with us.

Do We Share Your Personal Information?

As part of our normal business operations, we do not routinely share your personal information. However, we may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers strictly for operational purposes.

These third parties are required to act only on our instructions, maintain appropriate security measures, and comply with applicable data protection laws.

We may also share information within other business units of Shree Adhinath Merchantile to conduct business operations and market products or services, in accordance with our internal code of practice.

We may disclose information when required by law, court orders, or for the prevention and detection of crime, safeguarding national security, or complying with legal obligations.

How Long Do We Keep Your Personal Information?

The retention period for personal information varies depending on the purpose for which it was collected. Where legal requirements apply, we retain information for the minimum legally required period.

In all other cases, we retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

How Can You Update Your Personal Information?

If the personal information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you may contact us to request corrections. We will make the necessary amendments and confirm once the updates are completed.

How Do We Protect Your Personal Information?

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate organizational and technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or processing.

Personal data submitted online is securely encrypted, and sensitive information such as payment details is stored on secure servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on our website, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.

Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files that are downloaded to your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites function efficiently, remember user preferences, and improve overall user experience.

How and Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enable smooth navigation, remember preferences, analyze website traffic, improve performance, and deliver relevant advertising. Cookies do not store sensitive personal information.

Consent and Cookie Management

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings, although doing so may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party websites linked from our website are not covered by this Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
