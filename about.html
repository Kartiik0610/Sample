About Us

From Humble Beginnings to Towering Success

Our Story

Founded with a vision rooted in integrity and growth, Shri Adinath Mercantile & Exports Pvt. Ltd. has evolved into a trusted name in the import of stationery, hardware, and decorative products.

With over 25 years of experience, our journey reflects consistent progress, strong relationships, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Motto

“व्यवहार और आचरण में पूरी ईमानदारी” (Honesty in conduct and character)

Core Values

  • Trustworthiness – The foundation of every relationship we build
  • Innovation – Continuously evolving with market needs
  • Commitment – Delivering excellence with responsibility and consistency

Core Purpose

To make life happier by providing innovative offerings that add value to everyday living.

Who We Are

Shri Adinath Mercantile & Exports Pvt. Ltd. is a well-established importer serving customers across major metropolitan cities and emerging markets throughout India.

We proudly offer a diverse portfolio of high-quality products under our registered brands — JCC, ACCURA, HL, and SMART — each known for reliability, innovation, and value.
